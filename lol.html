Cast skills Q W E R Upgrade skills Ctrl+Q W E R (personally think it is more practical, when the hero is upgraded, there is no need to click the plus sign with the mouse) Summoner skills D F　 cast a skill Alt+Q W E R on oneself (such as Morgana's E)　Smart casting skills Shift+Q W E R (Smart casting means that when the hero casts skills, it directly casts on your mouse pointer or target, skipping the step of using the mouse to click and cast) In addition, Summoner skills can also be used to cast spells intelligently.　 This is the default smart casting shortcut key of the system, but it can be modified. Press Esc to pop up the main menu and select "Key Position Modification" to find it. Generally, it is directly modified to Q W E R, then your normal casting skills can be changed to smart casting skills.
